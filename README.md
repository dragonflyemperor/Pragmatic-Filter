# Pragmatic Filter

A powerful and lightweight Chrome extension that seamlessly hides social media posts containing specific keywords from your feed. Stop seeing content you don't care about on X (Twitter), Facebook, Instagram, and YouTube.

## Features

- **Seamless Filtering**: Instantly hides posts and comments as you scroll without leaving empty gaps in your feed.
- **Deep Scanning**: Looks through truncated text (like "Show more" buttons on X) and shared link URLs to catch keywords before they even appear on screen.
- **Search Result Blocking**: Automatically hides people, groups, and posts that show up in search results when you search for a blocked keyword.
- **Live Dashboard**: A clean popup interface shows you exactly how many items have been blocked on your current page and in your current browsing session.
- **Cross-Platform**: Works natively on:
  - X (Twitter)
  - Facebook
  - Instagram
  - YouTube

## Installation

This extension is currently installed locally (Unpacked).

1. Clone or download this repository.
2. Open Google Chrome and navigate to `chrome://extensions/`.
3. Toggle on **Developer mode** in the top right corner.
4. Click **Load unpacked** in the top left corner.
5. Select the `Pragmatic Filter` folder.
6. Click the puzzle piece icon in the top right of Chrome and pin the extension to your toolbar.

## Usage

1. Click the "Pragmatic Filter" icon in your Chrome toolbar.
2. Type any keyword you want to block (e.g., `spoilers`, `politics`) and click **Add**.
3. Browse your favorite social media sites. Any post containing your keywords will be automatically hidden!

## Privacy

This extension works entirely locally on your machine. Your keywords and browsing data are never sent to any external servers.
